How to start working with us

01

Acquaintance

If you order a design project for the first time, then you probably have a lot of questions and we will be pleased to answer each of them. You can text us at whatsapp, instagram direct or call us.
02

Tell us about your project

In order for us to make you a commercial offer, we need to find out the total area of your object, and what type of service you need - residential or commercial interior design or architectural design.
03

We will describe the process

What does the project consist of? What stages of work to go through? How will the construction take place? How much will the implementation cost? And there are many more questions that we will be happy to answer.
04

A meeting

If you are from St. Petersburg, then we make the appointment in our office. If you are from another city, then we are meeting via video connection. At this meeting, we will discuss in detail your future project and show the specifics of our work with examples.
05

Conclusion of a contract

When you received all the necessary information and made up your mind about how the work will be done, all you have to do is make a decision, after which we will sign a contract and begin creation of your dream project.
